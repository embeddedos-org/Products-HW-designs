COPPER WEIGHT RATIONALE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  L1 (1 oz):  Mixed signal top — fine pitch BGA fanout requires ≥75 µm trace/space
  L2 (2 oz):  GND plane — heavy copper for low impedance, thermal dissipation
  L3 (1 oz):  Power plane — carries 3.3V (500mA) and 1.8V (300mA)
  L4, L6, L7, L9 (0.5 oz): High-speed signal layers — thinner Cu = finer lines for
              controlled impedance without excessively wide traces
  L5, L8 (1 oz): Inner GND reference planes
  L10 (2 oz): FPGA 0.9V core — 2A peak at FPGA startup requires low-resistance path
  L11 (2 oz): Bottom GND — doubles as RF shield ground for antenna area
  L12 (1 oz): Bottom signal — miscellaneous routing, test points

================================================================================
  GROUND PLANE RULES
================================================================================

  L2 (primary GND):
    - Uninterrupted copper pour across full board area
    - Slot/moat between AGND (U4 area) and DGND (everywhere else)
    - Bridge AGND ↔ DGND at ONE point only: under U4 VDD decap
    - No signal traces routed on L2 under any circumstances
    - GND stitching vias: 0.30mm, placed every 15mm on board perimeter
    - GND stitching: row of vias every 5mm along edge of high-speed areas

  L5, L8 (inner GND planes):
    - Full copper pour; may have anti-pad clearances for via fanout
    - Anti-pad diameter: drill + 0.40mm clearance minimum
    - These planes primarily serve as EMI shields between signal layers

  L11 (bottom GND / RF guard):
    - Full copper pour, uninterrupted in RF antenna zone
    - Hatch fill NOT permitted (solid copper only for RF shielding)
    - Under-antenna area: no components on L12 bottom side

================================================================================
  SPECIAL FABRICATION NOTES
================================================================================

  1. Impedance control:  Order with impedance control on layers 1, 4, 6, 7, 12.
     Provide Gerbers with impedance stack-up table. Request TDR verification report.

  2. Via-in-pad (VIPPO): Required under U3 FPGA (VCCINT pads), U1 MCU (inner BGA).
     Specify: Copper-fill, electroless copper, planarize, cap with solder-mask.

  3. Material: Panasonic R-1566W or equivalent Tg≥170°C halogen-free laminate.
     Do NOT substitute FR4 (Tg 130°C) — FPGA power dissipation will delaminate.

  4. IPC class: Manufacture to IPC-6012 Class 3 (high reliability / military).
     Require AOI (Automated Optical Inspection) on all layers.
     Require electrical test: flying probe or bed-of-nails continuity + isolation.

  5. Radiation consideration: For flight hardware, avoid any laminate with
     boron-containing fillers (borophosphosilicate glass). Use standard E-glass weave.
     Specify void-free lamination (no voids in prepreg under BGA landing areas).

  6. Copper plating uniformity: ±10% variation max across board for via walls.
     Thin via walls cause opens under thermal cycling in space (−55°C to +125°C).

  7. Cleanliness: IPC-CH-65B. No-clean flux only. Aqueous wash after reflow if
     flux is not no-clean certified. Ionic contamination: < 1.56 µg NaCl eq/cm².

  8. Conformal coat: MIL-I-46058 Type ER (epoxy resin) or Type SR (silicone).
     Apply after functional test. Mask J1, J2, J3 connectors and test points.
     Coat thickness: 50–130 µm (per IPC-A-610).
